Organize up to 100 standard 3 mL vials in one durable, custom-made storage case.

This large-capacity vial organizer features a fitted 10 × 10 insert, a hinged lid, and two front latches. Each vial rests in its own individual opening, helping keep your collection upright, separated, and easy to access.

The standard insert has approximately 16.8 mm openings. Because vial dimensions can vary between manufacturers, custom opening sizes are also available based on measurements supplied by the buyer.

PRODUCT FEATURES

• Holds up to 100 standard 3 mL vials
• Individual openings arranged in a 10 × 10 layout
• Standard opening diameter of approximately 16.8 mm
• Custom opening sizes available
• Two front latches
• Hinged lid
• Choice of outer-case and insert colors
• Made from durable, lightweight 3D-printed PLA
• Printed and assembled to order

CUSTOM SIZING

The standard version is made with approximately 16.8 mm openings.

For a custom fit, please provide:

• Vial body diameter
• Vial height
• Measurements in millimeters whenever possible

A small amount of clearance may be added to allow the vials to be inserted and removed comfortably.

Custom sizing is based entirely on the measurements supplied by the buyer. Please measure carefully, preferably with digital calipers.

IMPORTANT STORAGE INFORMATION

This product is intended solely as a general storage and organization accessory.

It is not:

• Insulated or temperature controlled
• Airtight or waterproof
• Sterile
• Child-resistant
• A refrigeration device
• Intended to replace the storage instructions supplied with any vial or its contents

Always follow the storage and handling directions provided by the manufacturer or supplier of your vials.

Because the case is made from PLA, avoid prolonged exposure to excessive heat, direct sunlight, dishwashers, or hot vehicles. High temperatures may cause PLA components to soften or deform.
