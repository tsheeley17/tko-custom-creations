Your logo. Your design. Permanently engraved on a 20oz stainless steel tumbler built to last.

DESCRIPTION:
Whether you're outfitting a team, promoting a business, gifting a club member, or creating something entirely your own — this is your blank canvas.

Each tumbler is laser engraved to order with your custom logo, design, or artwork. The engraving process removes the powder coating to reveal the stainless steel beneath, producing a sharp, high-contrast result that won't peel, crack, or fade — no matter how many times it goes through the dishwasher.
No templates. No pre-set designs. Just your artwork, exactly as you want it.

What this listing is for
This is the fully custom option — bring your own logo, design, monogram, or artwork. If you're looking for a Masonic tumbler, groomsmen tumbler, or other themed design, we have dedicated listings for those.

What you'll need to provide
A print-ready or vector file works best: SVG, AI, EPS, or high-resolution PNG/PDF (300 DPI minimum). If you have a JPG or low-res image, send it anyway and we'll let you know if it'll work before production begins.
Send your file via Etsy messages after placing your order.

Color options
Black · Navy · Red · Green · Purple · Pink

Processing time
Orders are typically engraved and shipped within 2–4 business days after artwork is received and approved.

KEY FEATURES:

20oz stainless steel tumbler
Double-wall vacuum insulation — keeps drinks hot or cold for hours
Durable powder-coated finish
Precision laser engraving — permanent, won't peel or fade
Fits most standard cup holders
Dishwasher safe
Available in 6 colors
Engraved in-house at our shop in Ohio

FAQ:
Q: Can I send a low-resolution image?
A: Send it and we'll evaluate it. Simple logos and text often work fine even from a lower-res file. If it won't engrave cleanly, we'll tell you before we start.
Q: Can you design something for me?
A: This listing is for customers providing their own artwork. If you need design help, message us before ordering and we can discuss options.
Q: Can I order multiple tumblers with different designs?
A: Yes — message us before ordering for bulk or multi-design orders and we'll set up the right listing for you.
Q: How do I know what the engraving will look like?
A: We'll send a digital preview before engraving on request. Just mention it in your message when you send the file.
