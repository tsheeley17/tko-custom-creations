Keep loose batteries organized, protected, and easy to find with this compact 3D printed battery storage box. Choose from several interior layouts designed for AA, AAA, and 9V batteries, including single-size and mixed configurations.

Each battery rests in its own fitted slot, helping prevent batteries from rolling around loose in drawers, cabinets, toolboxes, classrooms, emergency kits, or travel supplies. The hinged lid and secure front latch keep everything together in one convenient case.
