Show your Masonic pride with a badge that's as sharp as your suit. These custom laser-engraved acrylic fold-over badges clip over the pocket of any suit jacket, making them a go-to for lodge nights, degree work, officer installations, and every formal occasion in between.

Badge Style Options
Choose from a standard badge or add a name slide at the bottom — a 3D printed slide-in name holder that lets you display your name separately from the main badge body. Name slides are available in white, black, or gold (PETG). Great if you want your name clearly visible at a glance during lodge.

What to Expect
These badges are made from quality acrylic with a laser-engraved finish. Because they're made to order, please double-check your personalization details before submitting — we engrave exactly what you send us.
Natural variation in the acrylic and engraving contrast is part of the handmade character of each piece. Once engraved, the personalization is permanent.

A Note on Fit
These badges are designed to fold over and clip onto a standard suit jacket breast pocket. They work well on most suit, sport coat, and dress shirt pockets.
