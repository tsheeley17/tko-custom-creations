Show your Masonic pride with a badge that's as sharp as your suit. These custom laser-engraved acrylic fold-over badges clip over the pocket of any suit jacket — perfect for lodge nights, degree work, chapter convocations, officer installations, and every formal Masonic occasion in between.
Made in the USA by a small business right here in Ohio by a brother, each badge is cut and engraved to order. No mass production. No generic designs. Just clean, precise laser engraving that's permanent — it won't fade, peel, or scratch off.
Ready in just 2–5 business days.

What you'll get
A custom acrylic fold-over pocket badge, laser engraved with your choice of symbol, name, and organization. Available with or without a title slide.

Standard badge — 3" wide × ~ 2.75 - 3" long — $11.50
With title slide — 3" wide × ~3.25" long — $12.99
(Length of badge depends on text/design)

Color options

Green, Blue, Red, Purple, Dark Green, Black — engrave to crisp white
Gold — engraves to bold black
White — engraves to red or black (your choice)

Craft Lodge · Royal Arch Chapter · Cryptic Council · Commandery · Priory · AMD · Knight Masons · Scottish Rite · Shrine · and many more. Have something unique? We can work with custom artwork too.
