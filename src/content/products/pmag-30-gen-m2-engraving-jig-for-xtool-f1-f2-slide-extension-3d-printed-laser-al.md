Description
Precision Engraving Made Easy

Speed up your engraving workflow with this custom-designed PMAG 30 GEN M2 alignment jig, made specifically for use with the official xTool® F1 and F2 Slide Extension.

This system securely holds a Magpul® PMAG 30 GEN M2 (5.56/.223) magazine in a repeatable position, helping produce consistent engraving results from one magazine to the next.

The included 3D printed slide adapter attaches to the official xTool Slide Extension using four removable clips. Once installed, the jig slides smoothly within the extension, allowing quick loading and unloading while maintaining consistent alignment.

Whether you're engraving one magazine or dozens, this fixture reduces setup time and improves repeatability.
