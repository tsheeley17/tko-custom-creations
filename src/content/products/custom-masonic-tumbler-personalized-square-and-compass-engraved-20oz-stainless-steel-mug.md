This custom Masonic tumbler is laser engraved with your choice of symbol and text, making it a perfect Freemason gift for lodge members, officers, and brothers. Each 20oz stainless steel tumbler is personalized and engraved to order.

Whether you are purchasing for yourself, a fellow brother, or as a lodge gift, this engraved tumbler provides a practical and meaningful way to display Masonic pride. The laser engraving process permanently marks the design into the coating of the tumbler, creating a crisp and durable finish that will not peel or fade.

These tumblers make excellent gifts for lodge officers, newly raised Master Masons, installation ceremonies, anniversaries, or other special occasions within the fraternity.
