Keep up to 20 standard 3 mL vials organized in a compact, durable storage case.

This 3D-printed vial box features individual openings for each vial, a hinged lid, and a secure front latch. The standard vial opening is approximately 16.8 mm, but the insert can be adjusted to fit other vial diameters when measurements are provided before ordering.

Features
Holds up to 20 vials
Standard opening diameter: approximately 16.8 mm
Custom opening sizes available based on your vial measurements
Individual vial openings in a 4 × 5 layout
Secure hinged lid
Front latch closure
Choice of box and insert colors
Made from durable 3D-printed PLA
Compact design for organized storage
Custom sizing

Vial dimensions can vary between manufacturers. The standard insert is designed with approximately 16.8 mm openings.

For a custom fit, please send the following before ordering:

Vial body diameter
Vial height
Measurement in millimeters, if possible

Custom sizing is based on the measurements provided by the buyer. Slight clearance may be added to allow the vials to be inserted and removed easily.

What is included
One 20-count vial storage case
One fitted vial insert

Vials and vial contents are not included.

Important information

This product is made from PLA. Avoid prolonged exposure to high heat, direct sunlight, dishwashers, or hot vehicles, as PLA may soften or deform.

This item is intended as a storage and organization accessory only. It is not insulated, refrigerated, airtight, waterproof, sterile, child-resistant, or temperature controlled.

Always follow the storage instructions provided by the vial manufacturer or supplier.

Because each item is individually 3D printed, minor layer lines, surface texture, or small cosmetic variations may be visible. These are normal characteristics of 3D printing.
